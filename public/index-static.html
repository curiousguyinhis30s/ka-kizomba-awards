Welcome to Dance Awards SEA

We are a community-driven initiative celebrating the vibrant Kizomba scene across Southeast Asia.

Our mission is to unite dancers, instructors, DJs, and venues by recognizing excellence and passion. Through these awards, we strengthen bonds, inspire growth, and celebrate the art that brings us together on the dance floor.
